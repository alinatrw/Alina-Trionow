BIO

Alina Trionow is a German artist and performer. She studies Fine Arts at the Bauhaus University Weimar, Performance and Scenography at the Universitat Politècnica de València in Spain, and Theatre Studies at Leipzig University. As a part of the collective SENSORIUM for performance she engages with art as a social practice.

In her work, she explores the poetic narrative potential of atmospheres and materiality through the media of painting, drawing, installation, and performance. Influenced by posthumanism and new materialism, she observes the incidental relationships between textures, objects, bodies and sounds, in which the human being appears as an equal part of a larger whole.

Alina Trionow lives in Weimar, Germany, and works between Spain, Italy, Austria and Switzerland.
