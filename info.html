BIO

Alina Trionow (Stralsund, 2001) is a German artist and performer. She studies Fine Arts at the Bauhaus University Weimar, Performance and Scenography at the Universitat Politècnica de València in Spain, and Theatre Studies at Leipzig University. As a part of the collective SENSORIUM for performance she engages with art as a social practice.

In her work, she explores the poetic narrative potential of atmospheres and materiality through the media of painting, drawing, installation, and performance. Influenced by posthumanism and new materialism, she observes the incidental, fleeting relationships between textures, objects, bodies, and moods, in which the human being appears as an equal part of a larger whole. Human presence is subject to the ambiguity and transience of sensations, as well as the influence of the environment on perception.

Alina Trionow lives in Weimar, Germany, and works between Spain, Italy, Austria and Switzerland.
