BIO

Alina Trionow is a German artist and performer. She studied Fine Arts at Bauhaus University Weimar, Performance and Scenography at Universitat Politècnica de València in Spain and currently Theatre Studies at Leipzig University/ University of Vienna. As part of the collective SENSORIUM she engages with postactionism and art as a social practice.

In her own work, she explores the poetic narrative potential of atmospheres and materiality through the media of performance, assemblage and drawing. Here, incidental relationships between textures, objects, bodies and sounds frame the human being as an equal part of a larger whole.

Alina Trionow lives in Vienna, Austria.
